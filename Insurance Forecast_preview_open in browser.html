Final Model Interpretation¶

The model explains 73,7% of the variance on charges.

Age, BMI and Smoker have a significant positive influence on charges. The effect of smoker is by far the largest.

The prior elimination of the region variables and the insignificance of most children dummies suggests no notable effect of region and number of children on number of charges. Only 2 children is significant here on an alpha=5% level, which may be an isolated effect or a fluke in the data. By effect size it has minimal influence.
